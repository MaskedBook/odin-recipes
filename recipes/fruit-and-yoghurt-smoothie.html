Fruit and Yoghurt Smoothie

I sure love smoothies!

And if you love them too, then this delicious yoghort smoothie is just for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Directions

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender;
  2. blend it lmao
